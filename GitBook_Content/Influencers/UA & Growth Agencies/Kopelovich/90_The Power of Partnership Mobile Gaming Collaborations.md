### Match Masters x Dots.eco

*Match Masters* has teamed up with [Dots.eco](https://www.linkedin.com/company/dots-eco/) for a unique collaboration that turns in-game achievements into real-world environmental impact. From January 23 to 28, players can take part in the Match Tree Community Challenge, where collecting virtual trees in the game contributes to planting real trees. Every tree collected helps fill both a personal and community progress bar, with milestone rewards unlocking real-world tree plantings.

The ultimate goal is to reach **15,000 trees planted**, and all participants will receive a certificate of impact along with a tracking option to follow the progress of their contributions. This partnership showcases how mobile games can drive positive environmental change, blending entertainment with meaningful sustainability efforts.

![](https://media.licdn.com/dms/image/v2/D4D12AQG3CM06WkUbjQ/article-inline_image-shrink_1000_1488/article-inline_image-shrink_1000_1488/0/1739131243600?e=1744848000&v=beta&t=BSwkYfHBdAU9MK4brlGiWuFuaFp1As0q2C1MsfE8RWQ)
