# Boosting Monetization: Season Pass In Candy Crush Soda Saga

**Candy Crush Soda Saga by King now offers players a new version of the season pass, allowing them to choose a paid pass that best suits their level of involvement and participation in the game.**

![](https://www.gamigion.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/image-65-498x1024.png)

**Monetization**

The introduction of the new season pass option enhances monetization for the developer. Previously, players had only one fixed option, paying $5.99 for rewards upon completing eight stages. However, with the new system, players can earn more rewards by actively participating in the game. Now they can choose seasonal pass with 7, 14, and 21 stages. Price starts from $4.99 to $12.99
