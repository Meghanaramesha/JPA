# Simple Games Can Be Influential

#### “Banana” shows us why.

##### I have seen skepticism about simple games. People say, “It’s just clicking a banana.”

###### But here’s the reality: “Banana” is a viral sensation.

![](https://www.gamigion.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/image-87.png)

###### This free to play clicker game on Steam, has captivated over 141,000 players.

**→ Its simple mechanic**  
↳ clicking on a banana  
↳ has created a massive following.  
  
**1. Banana Drops:**  
Players receive unique bananas every 3 and 18 hours in their Steam inventory, which can be collected, traded, or sold on the Steam Marketplace.  
  
**2. Marketplace Dynamics:**  
Most bananas are worth a few cents, but rare ones can fetch higher prices, creating an exciting game economy.  
  
**3. Community Engagement:**  
“Banana” has a large player base and encourages community involvement with user-submitted banana art.  
  
**4. Future Updates:**  
The developers will add customizable banana appearances, minigames, and a banana exchange feature.  
  
Some call it a “dumb game with bananas,” but the developers embrace its quirky nature, increasing its viral appeal.  
  
“Banana” shows how simple ideas can create engaging player communities and economies.  
  
*Accept the unexpected. You might find the next big hit.*
