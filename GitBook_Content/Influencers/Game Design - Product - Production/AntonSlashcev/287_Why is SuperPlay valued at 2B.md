###### Why is SuperPlay valued at $2B?

Playtika is acquiring SuperPlay for up to $1.95B, and here’s why:  
  
1. Key Facts:  
– Founded in 2019 in Tel Aviv, Israel  
– 350+ employees  
– Current monthly revenue: $22M (a massive +123% YoY growth!)  
  
2. Their games are crushing it:  
– Dice Dreams: 45.7M downloads, generating $17.6M in revenue  
– Domino Dreams: 10M downloads, making $4.5M in its first year  
  
3. Why the excitement?  
– Playtika’s deal values SuperPlay at almost 10x its annual revenue  
– SuperPlay is one of the fastest-growing mobile game developers  
– With Playtika’s expertise in live-ops and data-driven monetization, SuperPlay’s revenue is set to soar even higher  
– Domino Dreams can surpass their first hit, so growth potential is huge
