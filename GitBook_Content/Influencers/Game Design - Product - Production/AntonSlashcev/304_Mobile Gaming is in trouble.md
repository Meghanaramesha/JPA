# Mobile Gaming is in trouble.

**Monthly downloads and revenue for the top 10 genres are still lower than 3 years ago.**

![](https://www.gamigion.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/image-220-708x1024.png)

###### Revenue is down by 13%.

↳ Top genres like RPGs and Shooters saw big drops  
↳ Puzzle and Action/Brawl games are on the rise  
  
**Downloads have dipped by 4%.**  
↳ Arcade games took a hit  
↳ Genres like Casino and Sports are gaining momentum  
  
**Key takeaways:**  
  
• RPGs: -23% revenue  
• Puzzle: +17% revenue  
• Arcade: -19% downloads  
• Casino: +23% downloads
