###### Daily Bonus Do’s and Don’ts

Based on the [Playliner](https://www.linkedin.com/company/playliner/) research, here’s a quick breakdown of what works and what doesn’t in daily bonus design:  
  
— What doesn’t work: —  
 • Confusing progress bars  
 • Lack of clarity on the next reward  
 • Poor visualization of player progress  
 • Missing timers  
 • Distracting visuals  
  
— What works: —  
 • Clear headers that don’t distract.  
 • Progress bars for additional rewards as long-term motivation.  
 • Well-visualized progress, with the next reward clearly indicated.  
 • A big reward on key days (e.g., Day 7) to keep players coming back.  
 • Precise countdown timers for the next reward.
