# Layers of Game LTV

![](https://www.gamigion.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/image-205.jpg)

###### Layers of Game LTV

Here is a breakdown:  
  
1. Marketing   
How do you bring players into the game and who they are?  
  
– Target Audience: Who are you appealing to?  
– Relevant Creatives: Are your ads on point?  
– CPI (Cost Per Install): Are you acquiring users efficiently?  
  
2. Engagement   
How do you keep players coming back?  
  
– DAU/WAU/MAU: Daily, weekly, and monthly active users matter.  
– Playtime & Session Length: The more time in-game, the better.  
– Levels Per User: Are players progressing? This drives retention.  
  
3. Progression  
How do you guide players through the game?  
  
– Completion Rate: Are players finishing what they start?  
– Late Game: What keeps them engaged long-term?  
– Retention: Keep them coming back for more.  
– Churn Rate: Minimize players leaving the game.  
  
4. Monetization  
How do you turn engagement into revenue?  
  
– Conversion & Purchase Frequency: Get players to buy and keep buying.  
– ARPU/ARPPU: Maximize revenue per user and per paying user.  
– Ads Revenue: Can non-payers contribute too?
