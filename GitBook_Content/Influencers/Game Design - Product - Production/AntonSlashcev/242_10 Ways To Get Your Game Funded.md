# 10 Ways To Get Your Game Funded

![](https://www.gamigion.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/11/10-Ways-To-Get-Your-Game-Funded.jpg)

###### In recent years, I’ve secured funding for 6 different projects.

Here are 10 funding options to bring your game to life:  
  
—- 1. PUBLISHERS —-  
• Funding: $50k to several million  
• Terms: Revenue share, milestone payments, creative control clauses  
  
• PC/Console Publishers – <https://lnkd.in/eXpN_nve>  
  
• Mobile Publishers – <https://lnkd.in/eFrqyyWh>  
  
—- 2. VC INVESTORS —-  
• Funding: $300k to $10M+  
• Terms: Equity stake, convertible notes, valuation-based funding  
• List of VCs Investing in Games – <https://lnkd.in/eExR25kB>  
  
—- 3. ANGEL INVESTORS —-  
• Funding: $10k to $500k  
• Terms: Early equity, advisory roles  
• Top Angel Investors (Shizune) – <https://lnkd.in/edy-UVy3>  
• Playcap – <https://www.playcap.gg/>  
  
—- 4. ACCELERATORS —-  
• Funding: $20k to $150k+  
• Terms: Equity for funding, demo day presentations  
• Accelerators List – <https://lnkd.in/eHY5acAg>  
• Incubator List – <https://lnkd.in/ehW4qwPu>  
  
—- 5. CORPORATE GRANTS —-  
• Funding: $5k to $500k  
• Terms: Non-repayable funds, reporting requirements  
• Epic MegaGrants – <https://lnkd.in/eq-FTZsQ>  
• Indie Fund – <https://indie-fund.com/>  
• Games for Change Grant – <https://lnkd.in/egUEbe8d>  
  
—- 6. GOVERNMENT GRANTS —-  
• Funding: $20k to $150k  
• Terms: Application process, compliance  
• Canada Media Fund – <https://cmf-fmc.ca/>  
• UK Games Fund – <https://ukgamesfund.com/>  
• Tranzfuser – <https://tranzfuser.com/>  
  
—- 7. CROWDFUNDING —-  
• Funding: $1k to $1M+  
• Terms: Backer rewards, stretch goals  
• Kickstarter – <https://lnkd.in/eXVvTM6X>  
• Fig – <https://www.fig.co/>  
• Gambitious – <https://gambitious.com/>  
  
—- 8. BOOTSTRAPPING —-  
• Funding: $10k to $200k  
• Terms: Sweat equity, founder agreements  
Resources:  
• Bootstrapping Guide – <https://lnkd.in/eeniqRTS>  
  
—- 9. SPONSORSHIP —-  
• Funding: $5k to $200k  
• Terms: Revenue share, sponsorship deals  
• Fortnite UGC – <https://lnkd.in/e4uhqzz6>  
• Roblox Creator – <https://create.roblox.com/>  
  
—- 10. ALTERNATIVE METHODS —-  
• Early Access: Release an early version of your game to generate revenue.  
• NFTs/Blockchain: Sell in-game assets or tokens.  
• Steam Early Access – <https://lnkd.in/eNW8hHge>  
• NFT Games (Coursera) – <https://lnkd.in/eYerh_8J>  
  
Every funding path has its pros and cons.   
Choose what works best for your team and your goals.
