# Social Engagement Time in Mobile Games

![](https://www.gamigion.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/Social-Engagement-Time-in-Mobile-Games.jpg)

###### Social Engagement Time in Mobile Games

Based on the [Playliner](https://www.linkedin.com/company/playliner/) research, here’s the breakdown of social presence in popular games:  
  
• Zynga Poker (with Core): 85%  
• Monopoly Go: 70%  
• Board Kings: 70%  
• Coin Master: 67%  
• Dice Dreams: 27%  
• Animals & Coins: 18%  
• Solitaire Tripeaks: 10%  
• June’s Journey: 9%  
• Candy Crush Saga: 7%  
• Royal Match: 5%  
• Zynga Poker (without Core): 5%  
• Fishdom: 4%  
• Best Fiends: 0%  
• Solitaire Grand Harvest: 0%  
  
Statistics are based on 10-hour playtests for each game, during which all moments where the player sees avatars or names of other players on the screen were counted.
