# TOP 3 Long-term Retention Champions

![](https://www.gamigion.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/11/image-48.jpg)

###### Retention is king.

Here are the TOP 3 long-term retention champions, according to [AppMagic](https://www.linkedin.com/company/datamagic/):   
  
1. [HONOR OF KINGS](https://appmagic.rocks/iphone/honor-of-kings/989673964)  
• D90 Retention: 15%   
• Genre: Brawl/MOBA   
• Daily Active Users (DAU): 574k   
• Revenue per Download Tier-1: $37.5   
• Secret to retention: Constant updates with new heroes and events, plus a thriving competitive scene. Players stay for the dynamic gameplay and strong social connections.   
  
2. [ROBLOX](https://appmagic.rocks/iphone/roblox/431946152)  
• D90 Retention: 14%   
• Genre: Simulation/Sandbox   
• DAU: 33M   
• Revenue per Download Tier-1: $12.8   
• Secret to retention: User-generated content and social interactions. Players create, share, and explore countless experiences, fostering endless engagement.   
  
3. [GARDENSCAPES](https://appmagic.rocks/iphone/gardenscapes/1105855019)  
• D90 Retention: 13%   
• Genre: Match-3 with Meta   
• DAU: 4.9M   
• Revenue per Download Tier-1: $17.6   
• Secret to retention: A perfect blend of puzzles, emotional storytelling, and immersive garden decoration. Regular updates and rewards keep players motivated.   
  
What’s the takeaway?   
  
• Consistent content updates.   
• Strong social/community features.   
• A mix of creativity and progression systems.   
  
These aren’t just games—they’re evolving ecosystems.
