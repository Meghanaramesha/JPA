# Meeple Secures £500K to Digitize Tabletop Games

**Source:** https://www.gamigion.com/meeple-secures-500k-to-digitize-tabletop-games/

Newly formed UK-based digital board game developer and publisherMeeple Corphas raised£500,000 ($629K) in pre-seed fundingto accelerate its mission of bringing tabletop games to digital platforms.
Founded by formerMarmalade Game Studio senior programmer Matt Hilsonandex-Electric Square game designer Harri Elmer, Meeple Corp partners with publishers to digitize board games.Its debut title,Kingdomino Digital, is set to launch later this year.
Investors includeHiro Capital, The Mini Fund, and industry angels. Hiro Capital co-founder Ian Livingstone praised the team, stating that the investment supports their vision to create top-tier digital board game experiences.
CEO Matt Hilson emphasized the company’s goal ofexpanding game communities and advancing technologyto offer fresh experiences for board game fans.
