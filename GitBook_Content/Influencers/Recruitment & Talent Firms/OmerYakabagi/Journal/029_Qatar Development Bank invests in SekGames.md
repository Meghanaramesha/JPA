# Qatar Development Bank invests in SekGames

**Source:** https://www.gamigion.com/qatar-development-bank-invests-in-sekgames/

Istanbul-based mobile game studio SekGames will use the funding to expand its publishing activities and grow its gaming portfolio.
![image](https://mobidictum.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/sekgames_team_photo-800x533.png)
Founded byAli Osman K.,Serkan Çotul, andAlper Kocabiyikin 2020,SekGamescontinues to make a name for itself in the mobile gaming industry with its innovative projects.
Specializing in hybrid casual games, the company aims to deliver fun and immersive experiences for players. SekGames plans to use the funding to expand its game publishing activities and grow its gaming portfolio.
The studio, known for successful titles like Police Station Idle, Dream Hospital, Coffee Shop Idle, and Money Print Idle, has been selected for support under the Startup Qatar Investment Program. With nearly 2,000 applications from the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, Türkiye, and India, SekGames stood out for its innovative approach and growth potential, earning a spot among the 11 selected startups.
![image](https://www.gamigion.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/image-129-1240x186.png)
![image](https://www.gamigion.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/image-130-1240x452.png)
![image](https://www.gamigion.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/image-132-1240x490.png)
As one of just11 startups chosenfor theirinnovation and growth potential, SekGames is now poised for major expansion. While the exact investment remains undisclosed, the program offers up to$500,000in funding to launch operations in Qatar, with potentialscaling support of up to $5 million.
The team expressed gratitude to Qatar Development Bank (QDB) for their support and also extended their appreciation to their dedicated team members and business partners who have contributed to the company’s success.

Continuing its rapid growth, SekGames aims to make a lasting impact on the gaming industry and is actively seeking passionate game developers and talented professionals to join its expanding team.
