Released in early August, **Arrow Puzzle** has already climbed to **#7** in the ultra-competitive sorting genre. The game has reached **2.7 million downloads**, which doesn‚Äôt say much since it‚Äôs **almost entirely ad-monetized**. According to Sensor Tower, its **DAU is around 250k and rising quickly.**

Arrow Puzzle‚Äôs core gameplay is simple and is played by clicking arrows and clearing the board. Each level takes about **30-90 seconds to complete.** You can make two errors per level without failing, meaning you can make one error per level. It‚Äôs harder than it looks, believe me!

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!WtSF!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F03376280-7eb3-406f-a3cc-f7c7a75ff58b_945x2048.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!WtSF!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F03376280-7eb3-406f-a3cc-f7c7a75ff58b_945x2048.jpeg)

The game monetizes with Banners, Interstitials, and Rewarded ads. Banners appear after the 3rd level to help with early monetization. **Rewarded ads** appear as hints at the top of the screen as soon as you start to make errors, already **from level 10**. Interstitial ads appear after every 2nd level, starting on level 17. I estimate that the game is currently generating about **$14-17k/day in ad revenue**. A big caveat here is that the game is presently scaling fast.
